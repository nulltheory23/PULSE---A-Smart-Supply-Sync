About Us

PULSE is a local-level healthcare supply coordination platform designed to reduce shortages and wastage during disease outbreaks.

PULSE addresses this problem by connecting hospitals and clinics within a city through a centralized system. The platform provides real-time visibility of available and required medical supplies, enables facilities to raise urgent requests, and suggests nearby hospitals that can support them based on availability and proximity.

Our goal is to support faster decision-making, reduce supply wastage, and ensure that critical medical resources reach the facilities that need them most—when they need them.
